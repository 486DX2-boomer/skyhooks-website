WHAT IS SKYHOOKS?

The SKYHOOKS system is a reconfigurable fitness and training solution designed to collapse into a small area when not in use,
ideal for government and public facilities where space is at a premium or even in your own home.

INSTALL ANYWHERE

SKYHOOKS can be installed anywhere with minimal modification and investment.

SAVE SPACE, TIME, AND MONEY

SKYHOOKS is compact and collapsible for space-critical applications. Up to three different exercises can be configured ahead of time,
and a spotter can change exercise settings during a workout to continue the circuit training.
SKYHOOKS is an all-in-one solution that eliminates the need for a multiple exercise pieces for different workouts, reducing overall cost.

TWO SIMULTANEOUS USERS

Two people can use the same SKYHOOKS installation at the same time, increasing its efficiency even further.
